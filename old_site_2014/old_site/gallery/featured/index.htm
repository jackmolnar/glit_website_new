Featured EIT Student


James Pegan

“My name is Jimm Pegan. I’m from Findley Lake, NY have been attending school here in Erie for Multimedia Graphic Design for just over a year.


My focus this past term has been to hone my abilities using Maya to generate 3D characters and environments for use in my personal portfolio. I’ve been something of an artist my entire life and Maya allows me to bring my creations and characters to life in a whole new dimension. I look forward to a career in character modeling and animation as well as videogame design where, not only will my designs come alive, but people will be able to become involved in a world I have created.”
